# KRAFT Weekly Phases

## Phase I – Build the Working System (Weeks 1–4)

### Week 1 – Foundation (Jan 28 – Feb 2)

**Goal:** Basic skeleton exists.  
**Focus:** **DB1** (database schema), **FE1** (UI scaffold), **BE1-start** (basic CRUD for core tables).

---

### Week 2 – Connect Data + Simple Logic (Feb 3 – Feb 9)

**Goal:** Data + screens + first allocation baseline.  
**Focus:** **DB2** (DB_SCHEMA doc), **FE2** (Tasks screen), **BE1-finish** (stable CRUD), **ML1** (baseline allocation I/O).

---

### Week 3 – Real Reasoning + Wiring (Feb 10 – Feb 16)

**Goal:** Smart allocation and front–back connection.  
**Focus:** **ML2**, **ML3** (reasoning + hard rules), **INT1** (frontend ↔ backend), **DB3** (seed data plan).

---

### Week 4 – MVP Lock-in (Feb 17 – Feb 21)

**Goal:** Demo-ready MVP by midterm.  
**Focus:** **DB4** (support tables), **UI2** (results UI polish), final integration pass.

---

## Phase II – Prove, Polish, Present (Weeks 5–7)

### Week 5 – Real Data Allocation (Feb 22 – Feb 28)

**Goal:** Allocation runs on real DB data.  
**Focus:** **ML4** (read from DB, consistent JSON output).

---

### Week 6 – Reliability (Mar 1 – Mar 7)

**Goal:** Trustworthy system.  
**Focus:** **QA1** (testing, edge cases, validation).

---

### Week 7 – Story and Hand-in (Mar 8 – Mar 21)

**Goal:** Strong narrative and clean submission.  
**Focus:** **DEMO1** (demo video + walkthrough), **DOC1** (final docs), **FUN1** (celebrate).
